Our 5 differentiators (beyond basic requirements)
① AI
Quotation scoring engine
Composite score: price (40%) + delivery (20%) + vendor rating (25%) + past performance (15%). Highlights "AI Pick" with breakdown.
② Approvals
Value-based dynamic routing
Threshold-driven multi-level chain, not a static list. Escalates to backup approver on 24h absence.
③ GST
India-native tax intelligence
Auto-splits CGST+SGST (intra-state) vs IGST (inter-state). GSTIN format validator. RCM flag. GSTN-compliant invoice PDF.
④ Scorecard
Vendor health monitor
Auto-computed on-time delivery rate, price competitiveness, dispute rate. Green/Yellow/Red health badge per vendor.
⑤ Portal
Vendor self-service portal
Vendors get a separate login. View RFQs, submit/edit quotations, track PO status, download invoices — eliminating back-and-forth email chains entirely.
